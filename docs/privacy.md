# Privacy Policy

**FamilyFeast**
**Developer:** chalkyjason
**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

## Overview

FamilyFeast ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your family's meal planning data belongs to you, not us.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

The App collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its core functionality:

- **User ID (via iCloud):** When you enable CloudKit sync, Apple assigns an anonymous identifier to associate your data with your iCloud account. This identifier is managed entirely by Apple and is not accessible to the developer. It is used solely to sync your family group data across your devices and with other family members you invite.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No health or fitness data
- No browsing or search history
- No advertising identifiers
- No analytics or usage tracking data
- No crash reporting data sent to third parties

## Data Storage

All app data (recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, family member profiles) is stored:

1. **Locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework
2. **Optionally in iCloud** using Apple's CloudKit framework, if you are signed into iCloud

When using CloudKit, your data is protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption. The developer has no access to your data stored in iCloud.

## Third-Party Services

FamilyFeast does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's CloudKit, which is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Within the App, data is shared only between family members you explicitly invite to your family group via CloudKit sharing.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account for as long as you use the App. To delete your data:

- **Local data:** Delete the App from your device
- **iCloud data:** Delete the App and remove its data from iCloud via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage

## Children's Privacy

FamilyFeast does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is designed for family use and does not require account creation or personal information entry.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
