# FamilyFeast

**Collaborative meal planning made easy.**

FamilyFeast transforms meal planning from a solo chore into a fun, collaborative family activity. Every family member gets a voice with our democratic voting system -- swipe right on meals you love, and let the consensus algorithm pick dishes that make everyone happy.

Plan your week together, generate smart shopping lists from your meal plans, and cook with confidence using step-by-step instructions and built-in timers.

## Features

- **Democratic Voting** -- Tinder-style swipe interface with Borda Count consensus algorithm
- **Real-Time Sync** -- CloudKit keeps recipes, votes, and meal plans in sync across all family devices
- **Smart Shopping Lists** -- Auto-generated from finalized meal plans, organized by aisle
- **Cooking Mode** -- Step-by-step instructions with countdown timers and nutrition info
- **Dietary Profiles** -- Per-member restrictions, allergens, and cuisine preferences
- **Budget Tracking** -- Estimate costs per recipe and track spending

## Privacy

FamilyFeast is built with privacy in mind. All data is stored locally first, with optional iCloud sync using Apple's end-to-end encryption. No accounts to create, no tracking, no ads.
